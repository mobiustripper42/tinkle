## What happened (diagnosis, no code)

**Watchdog misbehaves — root cause suspect: BOD fuse never burned.**
- Symptoms: pump arms once (or never) after a quick repower; chip lands in a
  tripped state; SPA fault = **Watchdog**, clear refused (correct §14 gating —
  trip line held). Full 30s+ power-off always recovers it.
- Ruled out at the bench with the meter: ATtiny pin 8 on **3.3V** rail ✓;
  pin 1 (RESET) = 3.3V with the **pin1→8 10k present** ✓ (no glitch-reset);
  trip line (ESP32 pin 36) = 3.3V with chip out ✓ (pull-up intact).
- Remaining cause: buildup Step 8.0 burns only `lfuse 0xE2` — **hfuse left
  factory `0xDF` = brown-out detection OFF**. Quick repower lets the rail sag
  through the corruption zone without a clean reset → chip wakes wedged/tripped.
- Earlier red herring resolved: a previous stuck fault was **UnexpectedFlow**
  from the floating flow-divider input picking up relay/coil switching noise.
  Flow override (DEC-015) is now **ON**; sensor disabled until water.
